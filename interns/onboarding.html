Meeting the Team

Congratulations on being chosen to become an intern at Ceural! In the next few days following your acceptance email, you will recieve an invite to a special Slack workspace with fellow interns where you will be able to interact with your new team.




Meeting Schedule

An official introductory meeting will be set up through the Slack workspace that will be lead by a Ceural representative who will take you and your new team through Ceural's process, the awards, the conferences, the clients, etc. The meeting will feature a thorough presentation on the company's functionality as well as how teams are split and who gets to go from a Research Intern to a Lead Researcher.


This meeting will go also go over key Ceural Labs procedures, timelines, and general information about company models.

Research

    All academia from teams will be licensed under Ceural Laboratories ®. The names of all respective researchers will be published on the final research paper that will be either sent to conferences such as the International Artificial Intelligence Conference, AAN Neuroscience, Davidson, Google Science Fair, etc. Additionally, any client research papers will be sold to the client that we partnered with. For instance, we will be working with companies and universities such as Google, Nvidia, Intel and UC Berkeley, Stanford, and UCLA.

    Responsibilities:

    Interns at Ceural all work collaboratively on different projects based on the topics given. These projects and topics will be introduced through a presentation at briefing meetings.As an open-source research company, all research and related academia from each team will be available and thoroughly documented on Ceural's website. Ceural will apply these research papers automatically to a wide assortment of competitions and conferences, and if selected, the credits will go to the team who lead the research efforts and any other benefits that may come.

    Partner Program

    Ceural will be working and partnering with with major companies such as Nvidia, Microsoft, Intel, Google, etc. We will also be working with reknowned professors from top universities such as Stanford and UC Berkeley who will be working alongside respective teams to research new topics and potentially even build.
