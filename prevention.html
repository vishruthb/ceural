Brain Machine Interfaces

A BMI is composed of a brain and machine. The machine can be anything from a microchip to a bionic arm,
something that can provide sensory inputs from an external source and link it to the synapses
and neuronal information of the brain.

Education and Self-Regulation

Games and Entertainment
